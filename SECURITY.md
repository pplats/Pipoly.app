# Security Policy

At Pipoly.app, the security of our platform and the data of our users is paramount. We are committed to protecting our community from vulnerabilities and appreciate the efforts of security researchers and the broader community in helping us maintain a secure environment.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability in Pipoly.app, we encourage you to report it to us as quickly as possible. Please do **NOT** disclose the vulnerability publicly until we have had a chance to address it.

To report a vulnerability, please send an email to `security@pipoly.app` (this is a placeholder email; replace with actual security contact if available).

In your report, please include:

*   A clear and concise description of the vulnerability.
*   Steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
*   The potential impact of the vulnerability.
*   Any proof-of-concept code or screenshots that can help us understand the issue.
*   Your contact information, should we need to reach out for more details.

## Our Commitment

Upon receiving a vulnerability report, we commit to:

1.  **Prompt Acknowledgment:** We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours.
2.  **Investigation:** Our security team will investigate the reported vulnerability thoroughly.
3.  **Communication:** We will keep you informed of our progress and any remediation plans.
4.  **Resolution:** We will work diligently to resolve confirmed vulnerabilities in a timely manner.
5.  **Recognition:** We appreciate responsible disclosure and may offer recognition for valuable contributions to our security.

## Scope

This security policy applies to the Pipoly.app web platform and its official mobile applications. Any services or applications not directly owned or controlled by Pipoly.app are out of scope.

## Guidelines for Responsible Disclosure

When researching security issues, we ask that you:

*   Do not access, modify, or destroy user data without explicit permission.
*   Do not interrupt or degrade our services.
*   Do not engage in social engineering, phishing, or physical attacks against our users or infrastructure.
*   Do not use automated scanning tools that could generate significant traffic.

Thank you for helping us keep Pipoly.app secure!
