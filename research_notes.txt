Pipoly.app Research Notes:

Core Features:
- Microtasks: Quick data entry, categorization, verification.
- Paid Surveys: CPX Research integration.
- Micro-Freelancing: Writing, design, translation.
- Daily Challenges: Nim game (beat AI), daily polls.
- Games: Competitive Chess, Connect Four, Tic-Tac-Toe with wagers.
- Referral Program: Earn when referrals reach earning milestones.

Currency & Payments:
- Virtual Currency: Value (ⓥ).
- Rate: 1 ⓥ = $0.01 USD.
- Rate (Kenya): 1 ⓥ = 1.2 KES.
- Methods: PayPal ($5 min, 10% fee), M-Pesa (50 ⓥ min, tiered fees).
- Conversion: 10 Points (ⓟ) = 1 Value (ⓥ).

Technical:
- Mobile App: Native Android built with Capacitor 8.
- App ID: com.codeverix.pipoly.
- Website: https://pipoly.app.

Visual Style:
- Colors: Primary orange/coral (#E8693A), dark background, colorful cards (teal, orange, red, gold).
- Font: Serif for headings, Sans-serif for body.
- Logo: Stylized "P" in orange.
